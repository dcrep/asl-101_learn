    Signer A: Ask signer B if they are taking an ASL class
    Signer B: Answer the question
    A: Ask signer B where they are taking the class
    B: Answer the question
    A: Ask signer B the name of the class
    B: Answer the question

    Signer A: HAVE PENCIL YOU?
    Signer B: YES I HAVE. WHERE PAPER WHERE?
    A: PAPER THERE. HAVE BOOK YOU?
    B: NO, WHERE BOOK WHERE?
    A: DON'T-KNOW, SORRY

    Signer A: CLASS WHERE?
    Signer B: ME THINK CLASS THERE (approximate). YOU HAVE BOOK?
    A: YES, ME HAVE BOOK. NONE PEN ME
    B: OH-I-SEE. ME HAVE PEN. YOU WANT?
    A: YES! THANK-YOU!
    B: ITS-NOTHING

    Signer A: TAKE-UP LANGUAGE CLASS YOU?
    Signer B: YES, I TAKE-UP FRENCH. YOU LEARN SPANISH YOU?
    A: OH-I-SEE. NO, I NOT LEARN SPANISH. I LEARN #ASL.
    B: OH-I-SEE. CLASS WHERE?
    A: #RVCC. YOUR CLASS WHERE?
    B: SAME COLLEGE!

    • Practice:

    1. You are a good student.
    2. I like to learn.
    3. What is your favorite book?
    4. There are five students in the class.
    5. Yes, I understand the lesson.
    6. No, I don't want coffee.
    7. He likes to read books.
    8. They are students at the university.
    9. I have a pencil and paper.
    10. Why did you take ASL?
    11. I understood his explanation

    Signer A: TAKE-UP LANGUAGE CLASS YOU?
    Signer B: YES, I TAKE-UP #ASL. LANGUAGE CLASS YOU TAKE-UP WHICH?
    A: ME-TOO, ME TAKE-UP #ASL. GOOD CLASS?
    B: YES, ME THINK GOOD. YOU HAVE CLASS HERE COLLEGE?
    A: NO, ME HAVE CLASS THERE SCHOOL. YOU HAVE BOOK YOU?
    B: YES, HAVE BOOK. YOU HAVE PENCIL?
    A: NO, ME NOT HAVE PENCIL BUT HAVE BOOK
    B: COOL. HAVE GOOD CLASS!

    NEED ASK-YOU HELP-ME (I need to ask you for help)
    LATER GO-TO CLASS ME (I'm going to class later)
    WANT ACCOMPANY BOOKSTORE? (Do you want to go to the bookstore with me?)
    YOU-HELP-ME MOVE-THERE DESK CAN? (Can you help me move the desk?)
    NEED SHOW-YOU SOMETHING (I need to show you something)
    FINISH YOU-GIVE-ME BOOK? (Did you already give me the book?)

    Signer A: CAN YOU-HELP-ME? ME NEED A-S-L BOOK
    Signer B: SURE, BOOK ME-GIVE-YOU NOW?
    A: THANKS-A-LOT! WILL GIVE-YOU LATER
    B: ITS-NOTHING. NONE A-S-L CLASS TODAY
    A: COOL. BOOK ME-BRING-HERE SOON

    Signer A: EXCUSE-ME. YOU R-V-C-C STUDENT YOU?
    Signer B: YES! ME R-V-C-C STUDENT. CAN ME-HELP-YOU?
    A: PLEASE! TOILET WHERE?
    B: YOU KNOW LIBRARY?
    A: NO, DON'T-KNOW. ME KNOW CAFETERIA
    B: OH-I-SEE. COME-ON ME-SHOW-YOU TOILET
    A: THANKS-A-LOT!
