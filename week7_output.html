    Signer A: YOU KNOW MY TEACHER YOU?

    B: EXPLAIN MORE
    A: TALL. THIN.
    B: OH-I-SEE, ME KNOW WHO.

    Signer A: SEE WOMAN THERE, RED DRESS?

    Topics and comments

    SEE-THERE MAN EYES BLUE, HAIR BLONDE FRIENDLY HE (Oh, that guy over there with the blue eyes and blonde hair? He's friendly)
    THAT SODA, ME REALLY-LIKE (Oh, that soda? I really like it)

    Topic: (eyebrows up) -> Comment (eyebrows down)

    A coat with yellow dots -> Bring to me

    Perspective and Grids

    In ASL, you need to "mentally rotate" your view of the signing and take the signer's perspective
    Imagine the signer is looking at an invisible map that you must flip in your mind to comprehend the message

    Outline the grid clearly
    Choose your reference point
    One of the 4 corners of the grid
    This NEVER moves
    Starting at your reference point, show a clear path to the box you want
    No diagonals
    Do not cross your arms
    Emphasize the destination
