    Please flash the lights   [YOU, PLEASE FLASH-LIGHTS]
    Close the door. It feels a little cold   [YOU, CLOSE-DOOR. FEEL A-LITTLE COLD]
    Thank you. Please, sit   [THANK-YOU. PLEASE SIT]
    Go ahead and get started now   [NOW GO-AHEAD START]

    Signer A: DON'T-MIND FOR ME THROW-OUT GARBAGE?
    Signer B: DON'T-MIND, BUT CLOSE-WINDOW PLEASE
    A: YOU COLD?
    B: YES! ME PREFER HOT WEATHER
    A: HOT WEATHER(t), ME DON'T-LIKE. YOU COOL(t), BORROW MY JACKET
    B: FINE, BUT THROW-OUT GARBAGE YOURSELF

    Signer A: ME NEED PUT-AWAY CLOTHES. OPEN-DRAWER FOR ME?
    Signer B: SURE, ME-HELP-YOU
    A: THANK-YOU. HAVE CAR YOU?
    B: YES, NEED GO-TO STORE LATER YOU?
    A: YES, DRIVE-CAR CAN?
    B: NO, HAVE TEST LATER, NEED DRIVE-TO LIBRARY MYSELF

    Signer A: YOU LOOK COLD. WANT CLOSE-WINDOW?
    Signer B: YES PLEASE. DON'T-MIND BORROW JACKET?
    A: #OK. ME WARM ANYWAY
    B: THANK-YOU

    Requests: Use FOR-ME and DONT-MIND

    Would you mind opening the window?   [DON'T-MIND IT OPEN-WINDOW?]
    Okay if I take some paper?   [PAPER, DON'T-MIND I TAKE?]
    Thanks. And can you do one more thing?   [THANK-YOU. DON'T-MIND 1-MORE?]
    Can you throw out the garbage?   [GARBAGE THROW-OUT, CAN YOU?]
    Please put the box over there   [PLEASE FOR ME BOX CARRY-THERE]

    Make following requests using FOR-ME and/or DON'T-MIND

    Bring me the chair
    Stand up
    Take your book
    Write your name

    I am usually cranky in the mornings   [EVERY-MORNING ME TEND-TO CRANKY]
    I have class every afternoon   [EVERY-AFTERNOON ME HAVE CLASS]
    I worked on a paper the entire evening   [ALL-EVENING ME WRITE PAPER]
    I am thirsty everyday, so I drink a lot of water   [EVERYDAY ME THIRSTY, DRINK A-LOT WATER]

    A: ME PREFER READ. DON'T-MIND LIGHT TURN-ON FOR ME? CAN'T SEE BOOK
    B: IT'S-NOTHING

    Signer A: NEED CAR LATER. YOUR CAR, DON'T-MIND ME DRIVE?
    Signer B: CAN DRIVE. FIRST, NEED THROW-OUT GARBAGE
    A: #OK. OPEN-DOOR FOR ME?
    B: SURE. NEED CAR WHEN?
    A: ME WANT GO-TO FOOD^STORE SOON
    B: COOL. CAN BRING-HERE ORANGE FOR ME?
    A: FINE. REMEMBER ORANGE FOR YOU

    I am usually cranky in the mornings   [EVERY-MORNING ME TEND-TO CRANKY]
    I have class every afternoon   [EVERY-AFTERNOON ME HAVE CLASS]
    I worked on a paper the entire evening   [ALL-EVENING ME WRITE PAPER]
    I am thirsty everyday, so I drink a lot of water   [EVERYDAY ME THIRSTY, DRINK A-LOT WATER]

    Signer B: HUNGRY I. MUST EAT SOON

    Signer A: HOW FEEL YOU?

    Signer A: GOOD MORNING! HOW YOU?

    A: OH-I-SEE. SYMPATHIZE-WITH. WANT COFFEE, TEA?
    B: YES!

    Tense (Past/Present/Future):

    Time information should appear at the beginning to indicate past/present/future tense
    I will be going to the store tomorrow/I'm going to the store tomorrow   [TOMORROW ME GO-TO STORE or TOMORROW, STORE, ME GO-TO]
    I went to the store yesterday   [YESTERDAY ME GO-TO STORE or YESTERDAY, STORE, ME GO-TO]

    I have class tomorrow
    I had class yesterday
    I was sleepy before, but now I am hungry
    I will be cranky later
    I wanted a hot chocolate before
    I was taking 3 classes. Now I am taking 5
