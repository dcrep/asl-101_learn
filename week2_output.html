  • Personal vs. Possessive Pronouns

    • Wh-questions: Eyebrows Lowered/Furrowed (Example: YOU NAME WHAT)
    Yes/No Questions: Eyebrows raised (Example: YOU UNDERSTAND YOU)

    Practice:

    I want to try again
    How are you?
    You need to slow down
    It's nice to meet you
    I like the number 10

    Signer A: HELLO. YOU STUDENT YOU?
    Signer B: YES. I STUDENT I. YOU STUDENT YOU?
    A: NO. I NOT STUDENT I.
    B: NICE MEET-YOU

    Signer A: HELLO. YOU DEAF YOU?
    Signer B: NO, ME NOT DEAF, ME HEARING. YOU DEAF YOU?
    A: YES, ME DEAF.
    B: NICE MEET-YOU

    Signer A: HELLO. YOU DEAF YOU?
    Signer B: NO, ME NOT DEAF, ME HEARING. YOU DEAF YOU?
    A: YES, ME DEAF.
    B: NICE MEET-YOU

    Signer A: YOU STUDENT YOU?
    Signer B: YES. I STUDENT I. YOU?
    A: NO. I NOT STUDENT I, I TEACHER I. YOU DEAF YOU?
    B: YES. I DEAF I. YOU HEARING YOU?
    A: YES. I HEARING I.

    Signer A: YOU FROM WHERE?
    Signer B: I FROM #NJ. YOU FROM WHERE?
    A: ME-TOO. I FROM #NJ I.
    B: OH-I-SEE.

    Signer A: YOU FROM WHERE?
    Signer B: I FROM #NJ. YOU FROM WHERE?
    A: I FROM NEW-YORK
    B: OH-I-SEE. YOU LIVE WHERE?
    A: I LIVE #NJ. YOU STUDENT YOU?
    B: YES. I STUDENT.
